Programs

Understanding the German School System

The School system in Germany is unique and requires the active participation of both the parents and the school teachers to ensure that your child’s talents, strengths and abilities are identified and developed. With trained speakers from the Jugend Amts, you will be equipped with information which will ensure that you and your child have a stressed free experience in the school system. You will understand:

  • How the school system is structured
  • Early Childhood education
  • What is Compulsory education
  • The tertiary education system and
  • Qualifications of the higher education
Kindly send your name, and phone number with an indication of your choice of workshop to admin@kingscourt-leipzig.de to register.

Empowerment for Women

“When it is well with our women, then it is well with the family.” This workshop provides information on how women can further develop themselves and move forward in their careers, businesses and passions. You will be provided with:

  • Information from the Employment Agency and Job Centre
  • Language training opportunities
  • Information on where to get advice and support to fulfil your goals and dreams and
  • Opportunities for child-care while you train.
Kindly send your name, and phone number with an indication of your choice of workshop to admin@kingscourt-leipzig.de to register.

Understanding the German School System

Each year we have a considerable number of University students arriving in Germany most of whom will be confronted with the new realities of settling down in the German system. This Orientation workshop is to further help the students understand:

  • What you need to know
  • Where you need to go
  • What you need to do
  • As well as other resources and opportunities that could make your learning experience in Germany very fruitful.

This workshop is designed for both old and new students attending a university in Germany.

Kindly send your name, and phone number with an indication of your choice of workshop to admin@kingscourt-leipzig.de to register.
