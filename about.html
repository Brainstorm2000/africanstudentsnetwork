About

International students frequently face challenges prior-to their departure and upon arrival in the host country. Some of these challenges include but not limited to; language barriers, cultural integration, accommodation and other administrative and legal matters which makes it difficult for students to settle down in the German system

Over the years, we organize annual welcome meeting and periodic non-denominational workshop for new students as part of our efforts to support the integration process in Leipzig. Some of our areas of discussion include but not only limited to; empowerment for women and career development, orientation for African student, and understanding the German School System. To know more about our initiative, kindly visit our website: https://kingscourt-leipzig.de/empowerment-workshops.html.

Our interaction with new students have shown that if an international student does not have proper orientation before travelling to Germany, it becomes difficult for them to adapt into the new cultural environment throughout their stay in Leipzig. This turns to negatively impact on their academic performance.

There is therefore a need for proper integration process of student prior-to their arrival, upon arrival and a continuous interacting after arriving in Leipzig. The continues engagement seeks to serve as a stop-gap measure to propel student integration into Leipzig whilst serves as a top-gap measure to blend academic and social lives of students’ from sub Saharan Africa.

We create opportunities where students coming to Leipzig from Africa can network and access valuable information before departing to Leipzig, upon arrival, and throughout their stay in Germany.

Our Vision

Collaborate with existing sub-Saharan African communities in Leipzig to assist host institutions and the city authority of Leipzig influence international students from Africa’s perceptions, attitudes, and expectations to adapt to the new cultural environment they find themselves.

Our Objectives

To provide advice, guidance, and information for international students from Africa to ease their cultural integration process. Our initiative is therefore, structured to achieve the following sub-objectives:

  • Pre-departure integration activities
  • Arrival integration activities
  • Post-arrival integration activities
